SCIENTIFICALLY VALIDATED

MyGEN products analyze selected and scientifically validated genetic markers known to impact metabolism, exercise, nutrients, skin health and wellness. These markers have been studied in large-sized studies (over 400 people) and most of them have replication studies. Additionally, these markers have been carefully chosen because their effects can be modified using nutrition and supplements
